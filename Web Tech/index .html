Kya Re G Esports is a professional esports team based in India. It was founded in 2021 by Mayur Thube, a young entrepreneur and gamer. The team currently competes in the Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI), Team Kya Re G is not a part of the BGMI Professional League. Kya Re G Esports has a roster of 5 players.
